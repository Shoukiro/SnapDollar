Loan Advice from Millionaires

★
  • Debt is a tool. Use it to build, not to consume.
  • If the loan doesn't create income, you're paying to get poorer.
  • Smart people let borrowed money work harder than their own money.
  • Leverage grows wealth—but only with discipline.
  • Never take a loan to look rich. Take one to get rich.
  • Business loans are for multiplying, not spending.
  • Bad debt takes money. Good debt makes money.
  • Borrow for machines, not for mansions.
  • Poor borrow to survive. Rich borrow to scale.
  • Each rupee borrowed must earn more than its interest.
  • Income-generating loan = investment. Lifestyle loan = trap.
  • Smart loans replace your time with systems.
  • Don't fear debt. Fear not knowing how to use it.
In a nutshell: Credit is power—only if you use it with a plan.
