Loan Advice from Millionaires

  • 1. Debt is a tool. Use it to build, not to consume.
  • 2. If the loan doesn’t create income, you’re paying to get poorer.
  • 3. Smart people let borrowed money work harder than their own money.
  • 4. Leverage is how the rich grow fast. But only when used with discipline.
  • 5. Never take a loan to look rich. Take a loan to get rich.
  • 6. A business loan is not for spending—it's for multiplying.
  • 7. Bad debt takes money from your pocket. Good debt puts money into it.
  • 8. Borrow money for machines, not for mansions.
  • 9. Poor people use loans to survive. Rich people use them to scale.
  • 10. Every borrowed rupee must earn more than its interest. Otherwise, don’t borrow.
  • 11. If your loan builds a stream of income, it's an investment. If it builds a lifestyle, it's a trap.
  • 12. The smartest loan you take is the one that replaces your time with systems.
  • 13. Don’t fear debt. Fear not knowing how to use it.
In a nutshell: Credit is power—only if you use it with a plan.
