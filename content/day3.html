Read this before entering Stock Market

  1. Only invest the money that you can afford to lose.
  2. Greed and fear will destroy your wealth—control them.
  3. Don’t trade with emotions—trade with a plan.
  4. Time in the market beats timing the market.
  5. Start small, learn big.
  6. Never follow tips blindly—do your own research.
  7. If it sounds too good to be true, it probably is.
  8. Protect your capital—profits come second.
  9. Patience makes more money than panic.
  10. The market rewards discipline, not desperation.
  11. Diversify—never put all your eggs in one basket.
  12. Losing is part of learning—accept it, analyze it, grow from it.
  13. Invest in what you understand, not what’s trending.
  14. Quick profits come with quick losses.
  15. The market is not your enemy—it’s your mirror.
  16. Risk what you can manage, not what you fear.
  17. Read before you react. Learn before you earn.
  18. Don’t chase stocks—let your strategy lead.
  19. Focus on long-term wealth, not short-term thrill.
  20. Your goal is freedom, not fantasy.
