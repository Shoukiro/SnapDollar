How to Start a Business

★
  • Find a Problem to Solve
    Look around—what do people need or struggle with? Your solution = your business.
  • Validate Your Idea
    Talk to potential customers. Ask: Would you pay for this? Start small—test before going all in.
  • Make a Simple Plan
    What are you selling? Who are you selling to? How will you make money? What are your startup costs?
  • Choose a Business Name & Identity
    Pick a name that's easy to remember. Get a logo, brand colors, and a basic online presence.
  • Register Your Business
    Choose your structure (sole proprietorship, LLC, etc.). Get licenses, tax IDs, or permits.
  • Set Up Finances
    Open a business bank account. Use Google Sheets or apps to track expenses.
  • Start Selling
    Focus on getting your first customer. Use social media, word of mouth, or ads.
  • Keep Improving
    Collect feedback. Tweak your product. Stay consistent and learn as you go.
Tip: Don't wait to be perfect. Start small. Learn fast. Grow smart.
